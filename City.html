Herat province

Herat is one of the most important and historical provinces of Afghanistan, location in the western part of the country .It share borders with Iran and Turkmenistan , making it a key trade and culturalhub.

Bamyan province

Bamyan is a central province of Afghanistan , known for its breathtaking natural beauty and historical significance.It is surrounded by the Hindu kush mountains and is famous for being a peaceful and culturally rich region.

Badkhshan is located in the northeastern part of Afghanistan,sharing borders with Tajikstan ,China , and Pakistan .It is a mountaious province ,rich in natural beauty, rivers , and mineral resourse ,especialy lapis lazuli-asemi-precious blue stone historically famous worlwide.

Kabul Province

Kabul is the capital province of Afghanistan and home to the country's largest city, Kabul city. Located in the eastern part of Afghanistan , it is the plotical , cultural, and economic center of the country.
